Google Drive Transparency

How DocuScan OCR uses Google Drive

Clear, permission-first, and private by design.

  • 🔒

    Access only after you sign in

    DocuScan OCR only accesses Google Drive after you explicitly sign in with your Google account.

  • 📤

    Only the files you choose

    The app uploads only the documents you explicitly select for backup. Nothing is sent automatically.

  • 🚫

    No files accessed without permission

    No file in your Drive is read or modified without your explicit consent and OAuth authorization.

  • 🛡️

    No data sold or shared

    We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or documents with any third party.

  • 💾

    Used only for backup & restore

    Google Drive is used solely for secure backup and restore of the documents you choose.
