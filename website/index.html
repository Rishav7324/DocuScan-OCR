Why DocuScan OCR

Everything a scanner should be — and nothing it shouldn't

Powerful capture and recognition without the cloud lock-in, the subscriptions, or the surveillance.

Live edge detection

Real-time corner detection draws a guide over your document and auto-captures once the page is framed — no more crooked scans.

Real on-device OCR

The same ML Kit model behind Google's apps runs locally. Recognize text in dozens of languages with zero network requests.

Built-in encryption

Private folders use Android Keystore + PBKDF2 + AES-256-GCM. Encryption is the default, not a paid upsell.

Your own cloud sync

Back up to Google Drive, Dropbox, or Cloudflare R2 — uploads go straight from your device to providers you choose.

Portable exports

Save every document as PDF, DOCX, or TXT — open formats you can use anywhere, not locked inside one app.

Zero data collection

No analytics, no crash reporting, no ad ID. There's no backend server to receive your data — by design.

How it works

From paper to PDF in four taps

  1. 1

    Open & aim

    Launch the scanner and point your camera at a document. A live outline tracks the page edges in real time.

  2. 2

    Auto-capture

    DocuScan snaps the moment the page is framed, then perspective-corrects it automatically. Shoot as many pages as you need.

  3. 3

    Review & enhance

    Fine-tune the crop, apply filters (B&W, sharpen, grayscale), and run OCR to pull out the text.

  4. 4

    Save & export

    Your scan lands in an auto-created folder and exports to PDF, DOCX, or TXT — or syncs to your own cloud.

Privacy by architecture

Your documents belong to you

Most scanners upload your files for processing. DocuScan does the opposite: capture, OCR, and encryption all happen on your device. The only time data leaves your phone is when you connect your own cloud account.

  • No backend server, period.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or advertising IDs.
  • AES-256-GCM encryption for private folders.
  • OAuth tokens stored only in the Android Keystore.
  • GDPR, CCPA, and India DPDP Act aligned.
