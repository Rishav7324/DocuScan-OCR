Cancellation PolicyDocuScan OCR

Last updated: July 18, 2026

No Subscriptions, No Cancellations Needed

DocuScan OCR is a free application. It does not offer any subscription-based services, recurring payments, auto-renewing memberships, or paid accounts of any kind.

Because there is nothing to subscribe to, there is nothing to cancel. You are not required to provide payment information, and no charges will be incurred during or after your use of the App.

How to Discontinue Use

If you wish to stop using the App, you may simply:

  1. Uninstall the App — remove it from your device using the standard Android uninstall process (long-press the app icon > Uninstall, or go to Settings > Apps > DocuScan OCR > Uninstall).
  2. Clear app data — if you want to remove all local data before uninstalling, go to Settings > Apps > DocuScan OCR > Storage > Clear data. This will permanently delete all documents, OCR text, audit logs, and preferences stored on your device.
  3. Delete export files — if you exported documents to PDF, DOCX, or TXT, those files remain on your device in the Documents/DocuScan/exports/ directory. Delete them manually if you want them removed.
  4. Revoke OAuth tokens — if you connected Google Drive or Dropbox, you can revoke the App's access by visiting the provider's security settings (Google Account > Third-party apps with account access, or Dropbox > Settings > Connected apps).
  5. Delete cloud copies — if you used cloud sync, any documents uploaded to your cloud provider must be deleted from that provider's interface. The App cannot delete them on your behalf.

Auto-Renewal Disclosure

Not applicable. The App has no auto-renewing subscriptions to disclose.

Cancellation Fees

There are no cancellation fees because there are no paid services to cancel.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at support@prontly.in.
