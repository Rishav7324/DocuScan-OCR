1. Overview

This Refund Policy explains the circumstances under which you may be entitled to a refund when using DocuScan OCR. Because the App is provided entirely free of charge, the situations that typically trigger refund policies (defective product, service not delivered, dissatisfaction within a refund window) do not apply in the conventional sense.

2. The App Is Free

DocuScan OCR is free software. There is no charge for:

  • Downloading, installing, or using the App.
  • Accessing any feature of the App, including OCR, document capture, image filtering, encryption, export, and cloud sync.
  • Receiving updates or bug fixes (though no guarantee of future updates is made — see the Terms of Service).

You can download the APK from our official Releases page or build it from source — either way, the software costs nothing.

3. No Paid Features or Subscriptions

The App does not contain:

  • In-app purchases — there are no features, upgrades, or consumables you can buy within the App.
  • Subscriptions — there is no recurring payment for continued access to any feature.
  • Premium tiers — there are no paid or "Pro" versions with additional functionality that the free version lacks.
  • Advertising — the App contains no advertisements. There is no "remove ads" purchase because there are no ads to remove.

Because you never pay us anything, there are no refunds to process. If you acquired the App through a third-party distributor that charged you for it, that charge was not authorized by the developer, and you should seek a refund from that distributor directly.

4. Distribution & Support

If you are unsatisfied with any aspect of the App, your remedies are:

  1. Submit a bug report or feature request via our support channels.
  2. Contact support at support@prontly.in for assistance.
  3. Uninstall the App and discontinue use.

None of these remedies involve a refund because no money was exchanged.

5. Donations & Sponsorship

The developer may accept voluntary donations or sponsorship through platforms such as a donation platform or other services. If you choose to make a donation, you do so voluntarily and with the understanding that donations are non-refundable. Donations are not payments for goods or services — they are expressions of support for the project's continued development. Refunds for donations are handled on a case-by-case basis and are not guaranteed.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us at support@prontly.in.
